Julia's eggs

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tbs softened butter
  • 4 tsps water or milk
  • 2 tbs whippint cream
  • parsley sprig

Instructions

  1. in a mixing bowl, put eggs, pepper, salt and milk beat for 20-30 seconds
  2. take pan and smear sides and bottom with butter
  3. pour eggs into pan cooking over moderatly low heat
  4. continuously stir mixture for 2-3 minutes. then mix quickly taking pan off heat. cook until preferred consistency.
  5. pour butter and cream into pan.
  6. serve.
